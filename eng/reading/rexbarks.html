    • Chapter 3 - Kinds of Verbs
      • Intransitive complete v.
        • subject doing the action
        • no receiver of the action
      • Transitive active v.
        • subject does the action
        • have a DO revceiver
        • sometimes have a IO (indirect)
      • Trabnsitive passive v.
        • subject receives the action
        • RO (retained object): when IO becomes the subject of TP
      • Intransitive linking verbs
        • S + IL + PA (Predictate Adjactive)
        • S + IL + PN (Predictate Noun)
    • Chapter 4 - Dependent Clauses
      • Defn
        • Phrase: groups of words w/o a subject and verb; single part of speech
        • Clause: groups of words w/ a subject and verb
        • ex. When did Rex Bark?
          • Yesterday - an adverb
          • In the afternoon - an adverb phrase
          • When the mailman came - an adverb clause
      • Adverb Clauses
        • subordinating
          • introduces an adverb clause and joins it to a main clause
        • eliiptical clause
          • w/ some words left out
